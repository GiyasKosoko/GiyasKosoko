<h1 align="center">About Me</h1>
<p>
I’m a full-stack developer with a growing passion for both front-end and back-end development. I started out more focused on building clean, responsive user interfaces, but over time, I’ve come to enjoy the logic, structure, and problem-solving that comes with backend development just as much.

Most of my experience is in web development, working with tools like React, Node.js, and modern databases, but I’ve recently been exploring mobile development to expand my skill set even further.

I’m especially interested in building projects that create real-world impact—whether that’s helping people connect through shared interests, improving access to digital content, supporting vulnerable communities such as the elderly or those in need, or contributing to solutions to climate change and sustainability.

More recently, I’ve developed an interest in data analysis, and I’m excited to combine it with my development work to build more data-driven, meaningful applications.
</p>
